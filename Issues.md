Shared Bags: There's no way of knowing who your bags are shared with right now.

Gil: Gil amount should be per-bag so a shared bag should sync the gil amount to both players.

Shared bag trading: Players are able to trade items from a shared bag to another member of the shared bag. This removes the item from one player (the one that trades) but they remain for the one that's traded to, creating a duplicate (one in the main bag and one in the shared bag). If a shared bag item is traded to another player, the shared bag state should be synced for every member of the shared bag.
